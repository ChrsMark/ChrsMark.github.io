About me

Christos Markou is a Principal Software Engineer and CNCF Ambassador with deep expertise in systems observability, cloud-native technologies, and open-source software.

With a background in Electrical and Computer Engineering from NTUA, he has worked across various roles, from backend SaaS development to research in distributed monitoring systems.

Passionate about observability and the cloud-native ecosystem, he has contributed to widely used projects like Elastic Beats and OpenTelemetry Collector Contrib. Christos is an OpenTelemetry Collector Contrib approver and actively works on Semantic Conventions stability in systems and Kubernetes areas. Committed to open collaboration and community-driven evolution, he enjoys sharing insights through blog posts and speaking at conferences and events, engaging with users and the broader community.
